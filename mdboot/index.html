About Me

Hi my name is Zen, and I am a certified FISAF Personal Trainer, with certifications in CPR and First Aid and AED.

You have taken the first step towards achieving your fitness goals and I am here to help you. I was a competitive swimmer growing up, but a 2 pack a day smoking habit and weekly alcohol binges brought on a slew of health problems. I was diagnosed with Hyper Thyroidism and High Blood pressure. I was sitting 10 hours a day at my IT job and had chronic lower back pain, my muscles were so weak that I had trouble standing upright when I was washing dishes!

My turning point came when my dad suffered a stroke. I was shocked and worried that I would end up like him. I decided then that I had to make changes in my unhealthy lifestyle.

I quit smoking and embarked on getting fit again. It was really tough in the beginning and many times I wanted to quit, some friends even tried to get me to go back to my old unhealthy lifestyle. I realize now that it would have really helped me out to have someone encourage and guide me through that difficult first stage.

You might be feeling nervous or unsure about how to achieve your goals. I want to assure you that you will not be alone on this journey, let me be the one that guides you through your evolution to a newer better fitter you!
